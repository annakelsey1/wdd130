Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide adventurers of all kinds with thrilling and unforgettable white water rafting experiences. We aim to deliver adventures for our visitors while experiencing the natural beauty all around them. Our white water rafting trips offer exhilarating journeys that leave lasting memories and a deeper appreciation for the great outdoors

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our customers are adults between the ages of 18 to 65 with an adventurous spirit and love the beautiful outdoors. What do they want? Our customers want to experience thrilling white water rapids in gorgeous settings that they can remember for a lifetime. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device) Our audience will usually access our site via desktop or laptop, but we want to make sure they can still access us through thier mobile device as well!
